K2-98 b

K2-98 b is an exoplanet discovered by NASA's K2 mission, which is an extension of the original Kepler project. This planet is approximately 375 light years away from Earth and orbits a G-type star, which is similar to our Sun. Regarding its location in the Milky Way, K2-98 b is located in the Scorpio-Centauri arms, which is one of the spiral arms of the galaxy. This arm is known to contain a rich variety of stars and planetary systems.
