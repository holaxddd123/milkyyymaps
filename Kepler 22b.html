Kepler-22b

Kepler-22b is an exoplanet located about 600 light-years away from Earth in the constellation of Cygnus. Discovered by NASA's Kepler Space Telescope in 2011, it's roughly 2.4 times the size of Earth and orbits within the habitable zone of its star, Kepler-22. This means that the planet could potentially have liquid water on its surface. However, the exact nature of its atmosphere and whether it is rocky or gaseous remains unknown.
